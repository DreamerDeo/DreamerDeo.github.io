Longxu Dou 窦隆绪

I am a Research Scientist at Sea AI Lab, working on Natural Language Processing, particularly in multilingual LLM pre-training (Sailor/Sailor2). I earned my Ph.D. and Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Harbin Institute of Technology advised by Professor Wanxiang Che. I also worked as a research intern at Microsoft Research Asia with Jian-Guang Lou and at NUS-WING Lab with Professor Min-Yen Kan.

We are actively hiring (Senior) Research Scientists, Engineers, and Interns in NLP/LLM. Internship positions are available both onsite (in Mainland China, Hong Kong, and Singapore) and remotely. Feel free to reach out via email with your background and interests!
